From the histogram generated in Part 1.5, the approximate latency ranges observed were:

L1/L2 ≈ 100–120
L3    ≈ 120–220
DRAM  ≈ 260–300

L2/L3 threshold  ≈ 130 cycles
L3/DRAM threshold ≈ 240 cycles

Access times below ~130 cycles are likely L2 hits, 
     between ~130–240 cycles are likely L3 hits, 
     and above ~240 cycles correspond to DRAM accesses.
